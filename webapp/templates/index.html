Reducing Energy Consumption

We must find ways to reduce the amount of electricity we use. One of the ways we can do this is by changing habits around using lights.

There is a small amount of energy used in turning a light back on, but it is not as large as the amount used if it is kept on.

Turning lights off when they are not in use seems very simple, but is actually a habit that many people have difficulty with...

SOLUTION: motion sensor switches!

What's a motion sensor switch?

Pyroelectric InfraRed (PIR) Sensors allow you to sense motion! It's usually used to detect the presence of living things found in or out of the sensors range. Pros of these sensors are that they are small, inexpensive, low-power, easy to use and hold up to wear out.
